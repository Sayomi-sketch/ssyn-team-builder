Canyon Storm (CS) — Settings
CS: Team A takes the top (starters + subs) block sorted by THP — the strongest possible lineup. Team B gets the next block. If signups are short, the tool auto-fills with the strongest non-registered players.
Desert Storm (DS) — Settings
DS: After R4/R5 are locked in, remaining registered players are ordered by Participation Score. THP is used only to balance teams after placement. If signups are short, the strongest non-registered players fill in.
Auto-handled: R4 and R5 always start · Guaranteed players jump the queue · Left-out players auto-flagged as Guaranteed · P.Score influences DS draw order.

Universal Rules
★Left-out players are auto-guaranteed next round — tracked automatically.
★Subs with Delta ≤ −2 should be promoted to starters.
★Registration (the checkbox) is the #1 deciding factor each week. Rank + THP + P.Score determine priority after that.
★Participation Scores are fully visible — complete transparency.
Participation Score Key
+2Attended as Starter
+2Attended as Sub — showing up counts the same regardless of role
+1Signed up but left out (not their fault)
−3Signed up but no-showed — only applies if they were in the lineup
0Excused absence — life happens, no penalty
0Did not sign up — neutral

Only the last 12 weeks count. Older activity falls off automatically.
